Bio

Maxwell Norfolk a graduate student at Penn State University currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering. His primary research interests are in Database Systems and Compilers. His current projects include extending Microsoft's FishStore to use Just-in-time compilation for fast data ingestion. He previously attended Bloomsburg University, where he published an article into the Rose-Hulman Undergraduate Mathematics Journal.
